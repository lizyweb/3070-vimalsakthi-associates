ESI/PF Registration

ESI/PF Registration refers to the process of registering a business entity under the Employees' State Insurance (ESI) Act and the Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) Act. These registrations are mandatory for employers in India who hire a certain number of employees and are designed to provide social security benefits to employees.

Procedure For ESI/PF Registration

  • STEP 01

    Determine if your business meets the eligibility criteria for Employee State Insurance (ESI) and Provident Fund (PF) registration.

  • STEP 02

    Fill out the registration form with accurate information, ensuring compliance with all statutory requirements.

  • STEP 03

    After submission, the concerned authorities will verify the application and documents submitted.

  • STEP 04

    Once registration numbers are obtained, generate monthly contribution challans for ESI and PF.

  • STEP 05

    Maintain accurate records of employee details, wages, contributions, and other relevant information as per ESI and PF regulations.

Documents Required For ESI/PF Registration

PAN Card of the business entity

Certificate of Incorporation or Partnership Deed

Bank statement/cancelled cheque of the business entity

Rental Agreement, Utility Bill, Property Tax Receipt

Aadhar cards, bank account details

Memorandum and Articles of Association (MOA/AOA) of the company

List of Directors/Partners/Proprietor, along with their address proof

Form 5A (for PF registration)

Address proof of the business premises

Steps For ESI/PF Registration

1. Documentation Preparation

Our expert team will guide you through the preparation of the necessary documents. This typically includes gathering documents such as PAN card, address proof of the business premises, incorporation certificate, bank statements, and employee details including Aadhar cards, bank account details, and salary particulars.

2. Application Submission

Once the documentation is in order, we assist in submitting the registration applications for ESI and PF through the respective online portals. Our team ensures accuracy and compliance with all statutory requirements during the submission process.

3. Verification and Inspection

The concerned authorities will verify the submitted applications and may conduct an inspection of the business premises to assess compliance with safety and health regulations.

4. Issuance of Registration Numbers

Upon successful verification, the authorities will issue registration numbers for ESI and PF. The ESI registration number is known as the Employer Code Number, while the PF registration number is called the Establishment Code Number.

5. Challan Generation and Payment

We assist in generating monthly contribution challans for ESI and PF and guide you through the process of depositing the employee and employer contributions to the respective accounts within the specified due dates.

6. Renewal and Amendments

We keep track of renewal deadlines and guide you through the renewal process to ensure continuous compliance with ESI and PF regulations. We also assist in making amendments to registration details as required, such as changes in the number of employees or business structure.
