Increase Authorized Capital

Registering an increase in authorized capital signals a strategic leap forward for businesses, empowering them to pursue ambitious growth initiatives with greater financial flexibility. This pivotal registration process opens doors to expanded investment opportunities, fortifying the company's capacity to innovate, expand operations, and seize market potential. It reflects a visionary outlook, inspiring confidence among stakeholders and attracting potential investors eager to partake in the company's upward trajectory. With increased capital at their disposal, businesses can navigate dynamic market landscapes with resilience, driving sustainable progress and solidifying their position as key players in their respective industries.

Procedure For Increase Authorized Capital

  • STEP 01

    Convene a board meeting to propose and approve the increase in authorized capital. Draft a resolution to be passed by the board of directors.

  • STEP 02

    Call an Extraordinary General Meeting (EGM) of the shareholders to obtain their approval. A special resolution must be passed by the shareholders to approve the increase in authorized capital.

  • STEP 03

    Amend the capital clause in the company’s Memorandum of Association to reflect the increased authorized capital. This requires a resolution to be passed by the shareholders.

  • STEP 04

    File the necessary forms and documents with the ROC, such as Form SH-7, within the prescribed time frame. Attach the board resolution, special resolution, and altered MOA.

  • STEP 05

    Pay the requisite fee to the ROC based on the amount of the increased authorized capital. Upon successful verification, the ROC will update the company’s records to reflect the increased authorized capital.

Steps For Increase Authorized Capital

1. Board Resolution

Purpose: Approve increase in authorized capital.
How to Obtain: Hold a board meeting.
Document Required: Board meeting minutes.

2. Shareholders' Approval

Purpose: Obtain consent from shareholders.
How to Obtain: Conduct a general meeting.
Document Required: Shareholders' resolution.

3. Alteration of Memorandum of Association

Purpose: Amend authorized capital clause.
How to Obtain: File amendment with ROC.
Document Required: Altered Memorandum of Association.

4. Filing Form SH-7

Purpose: Officially register the capital increase.
How to Obtain: Submit form SH-7 to ROC.
Document Required: Form SH-7, board resolution, altered MOA.

5. Payment of Stamp Duty

Purpose: Fulfill legal stamp duty obligations.
How to Obtain: Pay applicable stamp duty.
Document Required: Stamp duty receipt.

6. Update Statutory Registers

Purpose: Ensure legal compliance.
How to Obtain: Record changes in statutory registers.
Document Required: Updated statutory registers.
